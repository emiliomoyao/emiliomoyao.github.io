About

Emilio Moyao is a performer and composer from the Los Angeles area. Having started playing guitar as a child, Emilio has gone on to explore different areas which include but are not limited to improvisational music, works in electronic music, and orchestral works.
Emilio is attending California Institue of the Arts, where he we will recieve a BFA in Music.
